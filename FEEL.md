# FEEL.md — Iron Canvas Design Agency Site

## Emotional Target
When someone lands on Iron Canvas, they should feel like they've walked into a master blacksmith's studio — not a sterile SaaS homepage. The craft is visible. The precision is tangible. Every element communicates: "These people obsess over details you didn't even know existed."

## Target User
Creative directors, startup founders, and brand owners who've seen enough AI-generated websites to know the difference — and want something that makes their competitors wonder who built it.

## Emotions to Evoke
1. **Awe** — "This is a level above what I've seen"
2. **Trust** — "These people clearly know what they're doing"
3. **Aspiration** — "I want MY brand to feel like this"
4. **Warmth** — Premium without being cold or corporate
5. **Kinetic Energy** — Everything feels alive, intentional, moving

## Must NOT Feel Like
1. Corporate SaaS template (Stripe/Linear clone)
2. Cheap agency with stock photos
3. AI-generated purple gradient slop
4. Overly minimalist (empty without purpose)
5. "We built this in a weekend" energy

## Visual Language Keywords
Forged, cinematic, editorial, warm metallics, haptic depth, tinted darkness, gold-on-obsidian, kinetic precision, analog warmth in digital space

## Selected Archetypes
- **Vibe:** Ethereal Glass (deep OLED black, radial mesh gradients, vantablack surfaces) BLENDED with Editorial Luxury (warm tones via gold accent, film grain, physical feel)
- **Layout:** Asymmetrical Bento (masonry-like CSS Grid with varying card sizes) + Editorial Split (massive typography left, interactive elements right)
